Featured Cocktails

New Amsterdam Vodka

A refreshing blend of New Amsterdam Vodka, muddled strawberries, and a hint of basil. Sip this smooth and invigorating concoction over ice, garnished with a strawberry slice and a sprig of basil, and embrace the spirit of summer. Cheers to the 'New Amsterdamn'!

Dan F*ckin Marino

A refreshing blend of cantaloupe-infused mezcal, Campari, and vermouth. Served over ice with a citrus twist or cantaloupe garnish, it's the perfect summer sipper. Cheers to sunny days and ripe cantaloupe!

New Wave Negroni

The New Wave Negroni is a refreshing and slightly bittersweet variation of the classic Negroni, with Aperol adding a touch of citrus and the Lillet Blanc providing a smooth, floral note. It's a perfect choice for those looking to try a modern twist on a timeless cocktail.
